# Commercial and post-market operations

## Intended use and prohibited claims

Intended use is local, consented, single-participant wellness-oriented ambient sensing within a qualified operating envelope, limited to capabilities explicitly promoted and enabled by signed policy. Until readiness approval, the system is an engineering candidate only. Medical diagnosis/treatment/monitoring, emergency alarms, covert sensing, identity inference, employment/insurance/education/law-enforcement decisions, deception detection, thought/intent reading, and unsupported affect/cognitive claims are prohibited.

## Jurisdiction, legal, regulatory, and privacy gate

The release record must identify each market, product classification, claim set, data role, biometric/privacy/consumer obligations, radio/firmware obligations, accessibility requirements, warranty rules, retention/erasure duties, incident deadlines, and named qualified counsel/reviewer approval. Missing, expired, conditional, or scope-mismatched approval blocks that market. No such approval is currently represented by this repository.

## Quality records and change control

Retain design inputs/outputs, risk controls, verification, traceability, suppliers, SBOMs, model/dataset cards, calibration, nonconformities, deviations, complaints, CAPA, releases, field actions, training, and approvals under versioned retention policy. Every hardware, firmware, model, policy, claim, market, supplier, or operating-envelope change receives impact review and requalification.

## Warranty, repair, replacement, and returns

Before sale, publish jurisdiction-reviewed warranty duration/exclusions and lawful remedies. Define triage, RMA identity, secure transport, encrypted-data handling, repair authorization, parts traceability, post-repair safety/site checks, replacement provisioning, cryptographic erasure, refurbish/disposal controls, turnaround targets, and return-root-cause analysis. Support must not request raw sensing payloads by default.

## Support and update windows

Declare supported hardware revisions, OS/firmware/software/model combinations, security-update duration, feature-support duration, response targets, offline update media, rollback constraints, vulnerability intake, and end dates. Signed updates promote identical tested artifacts; unsupported or revoked combinations fail closed.

## Installation qualification

Only trained installers may issue signed acceptance after every commissioning check and per-capability coverage gate passes. Room, AP/link, geometry, hardware, firmware, placement, calibration, interference, or material environmental change triggers requalification. An unqualified site cannot enable a capability.

## Incident, recall, and field action

Maintain 24/7 security/safety contacts appropriate to the market, severity triage, containment and capability suspension, evidence preservation, legal notification clocks, customer/participant communication, remote/offline remediation, recall/repair/replace criteria, effectiveness checks, regulator coordination, and closure approval. Exercises and real incidents create immutable records.

## End of life and decommissioning

Publish notice periods, final update dates, data export/deletion options, key destruction, device identity retirement, update-signing/key retention, vulnerability handling after sale/support end, spare-parts policy, recycling/disposal, and documentation availability. Decommissioning must visibly disable sensing and make governed payload recovery infeasible.

## Complaints and privacy requests

Accept accessible complaints and access/correction/deletion/withdrawal requests; authenticate proportionately; avoid collecting unnecessary sensitive data; assign case IDs; meet jurisdictional deadlines; propagate deletion across stores/indexes/backups where required; document exceptions; trend hazards; and link reportable issues to incident/CAPA review. Retaliation and high-impact use are prohibited.

## Optional telemetry

Telemetry is absent by default. Any future telemetry needs a new ADR, explicit opt-in and purpose, minimization, no raw CSI/features/identity/claims, local preview, retention/deletion, endpoint and key governance, DPIA/legal review, offline-safe behavior, and validation that disabling it changes no core function.

## Post-market surveillance, CAPA, and capability suspension

Trend installation success, coverage/abstention, faults, thermal/power/storage health, update outcomes, complaints, returns, support burden, security findings, misuse, and claim confusion using privacy-minimized denominators. Predetermine alert thresholds and review cadence. CAPA records problem statement, containment, root cause, scope, correction/prevention, owner, due date, verification of effectiveness, and closure approval. A capability is suspended immediately for revoked/expired evidence or policy, integrity/calibration drift, unacceptable error/coverage/failure, safety/security/legal finding, unsupported site/hardware, or failed field-action effectiveness; restoration requires new immutable evidence and approval.
