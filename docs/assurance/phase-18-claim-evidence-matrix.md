|---|---|---|
| Local, offline Rust-first operation | Architecture, runtime, replay, and sidecar-absence tests | Engineering claim only; hardware release evidence pending |

| Valence, stress, emotion, decision phase, thoughts, intent | No supporting evidence and prohibited/unsupported scope | Must not claim or expose |
| Medical diagnosis, treatment, monitoring, alarm, apnea/cardiac detection | No regulatory program or clinical evidence | Prohibited |
| Employment, insurance, education, deception, law-enforcement scoring | Prohibited-use policy | Prohibited |
| CrowPi/reference-Pi qualified product | Simulator/current-host evidence only | Must not claim; physical HIL/site/release gates pending |
| Commercially viable/production ready | Pilot, legal, manufacturing, support, HIL/soak, pen/fuzz evidence incomplete | Must not claim |

Every packaging, UI, API, sales, support, and training statement must map to a promoted capability and exact evidence bundle. Silence or “wellness/research” wording cannot cure functionality that creates a regulated or unsupported claim.
