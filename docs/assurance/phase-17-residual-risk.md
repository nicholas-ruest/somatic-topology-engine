# Phase 17 residual-risk review

Production residual risk is not accepted at this phase. The major open risks are physical-platform behavior under voltage, temperature, storage, radio, bus, and long-duration load; incomplete penetration/fuzz evidence; operational update/recovery/incident readiness; uncompleted human validation for most capabilities; unqualified CrowPi/site profiles; and absent manufacturing/support field evidence.

Software controls reduce but do not close these risks: fail-closed authorization, abstention, immutable evidence, deterministic projections, encrypted storage, bounded parsers, supervised optional tasks, signed packages/policies/acceptance, rollback, and explicit disabled capabilities. Owners and acceptance authorities remain those named in the hazard register and assurance ownership records. No open high-severity or unacceptable residual risk may be exception-accepted merely to meet a schedule.
