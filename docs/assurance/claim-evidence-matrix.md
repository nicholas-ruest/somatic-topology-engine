# Claim–Evidence Matrix

**Record ID:** STE-QMS-CLAIM-001
**Owner:** Product & Regulatory Lead
**Evidence authority:** Scientific Validation Lead
**Release gate authority:** Quality Lead

Every externally visible statement—including UI labels, API field names, packaging, demonstrations, support scripts, documentation, and sales speech—is a claim. An unlisted claim is prohibited. A row is publishable only when its status is `Approved`, every evidence identifier resolves to an immutable quality record, the stated release/hardware/model/market scope matches, and all required approvers have signed.

## Minimum evidence quality

Evidence must be generated by a versioned procedure, identify inputs and environment, retain raw results or content digests, state pass/fail thresholds selected before execution, record deviations and negative results, and be independently reviewed by a role other than its producer. Screenshots, demonstrations, issue closure, code presence, self-attestation, or a passing unit test alone cannot support a scientific, safety, security, or production claim.

## Approval record

An approval entry records `claim_id`, exact wording and locale, surface, jurisdiction, release/model/hardware/configuration digests, evidence record IDs, limitations, reviewer names/roles, decision, signature, and UTC time. Modified wording creates a new revision and requires reassessment.
